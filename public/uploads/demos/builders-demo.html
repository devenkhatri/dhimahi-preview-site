1

A builder or developer lands on the pre-rendered persona page from Google, a direct share, or a campaign link.

+0.0s
Behind the scenes

2
Persona Content

The page opens with the builders narrative, an infographic, and a direct explanation of why better digital presentation matters.

+4.0s
Behind the scenes

3
Trust Builders

Related insights, social sharing, reading-time cues, and structured content keep the visitor moving instead of bouncing.

+45.0s
Behind the scenes

The repo maps builders to relevant insight tags like real estate, construction, lead generation, and project management.

4
Consultation Path

The buyer clicks the consultation CTA. Calendly is attempted first so the fastest path is immediate self-booking.

+75.0s
Behind the scenes

The embed appends UTM parameters and branded styling, then waits up to 8 seconds before deciding whether to fall back.

5
Fallback Capture

If Calendly is blocked or slow, the multi-step consultation form appears and posts the lead to Netlify Forms instead of losing the inquiry.

+83.0s
Behind the scenes

The form captures business type, company size, challenge, urgency, budget, and preferred meeting slot, then tracks completion as a funnel event.

Why These Tools?

Each choice in this repo trades complexity for speed, editability, or resilience. The point is not trendiness. The point is fewer blocked launches and cleaner lead capture.

⚡

Next.js

Fast Delivery Layer

Pages are statically generated, so builder-focused landing pages load fast, rank cleanly, and feel finished instead of assembled on demand.

vs. a page-builder stack: Stronger performance, fewer plugin dependencies, and more predictable publishing.
🗂️

Markdown + YAML Content

Content Engine

Persona stories, service pages, settings, and supporting resources live in plain files, which keeps updates cheap and reviewable.

vs. storing content in code: Marketing can update messaging without waiting for component rewrites.
✍️

Decap CMS + Netlify Identity

Editor Workflow

Gives non-developers a safer editing path while keeping the final site under versioned control.

vs. ad-hoc file edits: Clear roles, invite-only access, and fewer content bottlenecks.
📮

Netlify Forms + Calendly Fallback

Lead Capture

The best lead path is self-booking. The safe path is fallback form capture. This implementation supports both without adding a custom backend.

vs. only embedding a scheduler: You still capture intent when embeds fail, ad blockers interfere, or slow networks get in the way.
