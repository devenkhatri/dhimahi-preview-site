What Happens When Something Goes Wrong?

We proactively address concerns before you have to ask.

Payment Gateway Downtime

What happens:

Customer's payment cannot be processed at that moment.

How the system responds:

Payment is queued automatically. Customer sees a friendly message. You receive instant notification. Retry happens every 5 minutes for 2 hours.

What the customer sees:

"We're experiencing high demand. Your order is saved — we'll notify you when payment can be completed." Nothing is lost.
