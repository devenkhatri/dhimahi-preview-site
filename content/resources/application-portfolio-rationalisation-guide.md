# Application Portfolio Rationalisation Service Guide

This guide provides a comprehensive framework for evaluating your current application landscape. It covers:

1.  **Assessment Methodologies**: How to inventory and categorize your applications (TIME model: Tolerate, Invest, Migrate, Eliminate).
2.  **Cost Analysis**: Identifying hidden maintenance costs and licensing redundancies.
3.  **Strategic Alignment**: Ensuring every application supports your current and future business objectives.
4.  **Roadmap Development**: Creating a step-by-step plan for retirement, consolidation, or modernization.

Download this guide to start decluttering your digital environment and focusing your IT budget on innovation rather than maintenance.
