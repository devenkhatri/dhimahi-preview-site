## Project Overview

Atithi Hotel Group, operating 4 properties across Gujarat with a combined 280 rooms, was struggling with communication overload. Their WhatsApp Business account, which had become the primary booking inquiry channel, was receiving 500+ messages daily with response times stretching to 4+ hours during peak season. They needed AI automation to handle the volume while improving guest experience.

## The Challenge

### Communication Bottleneck
- **500+ daily messages** across WhatsApp, with new queries every 2 minutes during peak times
- **4+ hour average response** time despite 3 staff members dedicated to WhatsApp
- **Repetitive queries** (room rates, availability, amenities) consuming 80% of inquiry volume
- **After-hours coverage** non-existent, losing international and late-night bookings

### Revenue Impact
- **30% of inquiries** abandoned due to slow response
- **High OTA dependency** (75% bookings through third-party platforms with 15-20% commission)
- **No quote automation** — each pricing request manually calculated
- **Group bookings** requiring 2-3 days for custom quote preparation

## Our Solution

### AI Chatbot Development
- Built conversational AI using GPT with custom training on hotel-specific FAQs
- Implemented natural language understanding for English, Hindi, and Gujarati
- Created intelligent intent detection for 15+ common query types
- Designed personality matching the hotel brand's warm hospitality tone

### PMS Integration
- Connected chatbot to Property Management System via APIs
- Enabled real-time room availability and dynamic pricing display
- Automated booking creation with instant confirmation
- Integrated Razorpay for secure in-chat payment processing

### Smart Escalation System
- Defined rules for automatic handoff to human agents (complex requests, complaints, VIP guests)
- Built agent dashboard showing conversation history and guest context
- Created priority queuing based on booking value and guest loyalty status
- Implemented seamless human-to-AI handback after resolution

## Results

### Automation & Efficiency
- **80% of queries** resolved entirely by AI without human intervention
- **Response time** reduced from 4+ hours to under 30 seconds, 24/7
- **Front desk call volume** decreased by 65%
- **Staff redeployed** from WhatsApp management to premium guest services

### Revenue Impact
- **40% increase** in direct bookings (reducing OTA dependency)
- **15% commission savings** on converted direct bookings
- **24/7 booking capability** capturing international and after-hours revenue
- **Group booking quotes** generated in minutes instead of days

### Guest Experience
- **Multi-language support** delighting diverse guest base
- **Instant responses** improving first impression before arrival
- **Personalised recommendations** for local attractions and dining
- **Post-stay feedback** automated through the same WhatsApp channel
