## Project Overview

Vasstra by Priya, a homegrown ethnic wear brand known for hand-embroidered kurtas and dupattas, had built a loyal customer base through word-of-mouth in Ahmedabad. Founder Priya Vaghela knew her products could appeal nationally but had no idea how to break into the digital space. She approached us to transform her brand from a local favourite to a national e-commerce success.

### Near-Zero Digital Presence
- **Website**: Basic Shopify store with 200 monthly visitors, no SEO, and poor mobile experience
- **Social media**: 800 Instagram followers with irregular, unplanned posting
- **Email list**: No customer database or email marketing
- **Advertising**: Zero experience with paid digital channels
